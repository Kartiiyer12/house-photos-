About This Property

A Bright, Modern Home with Everything on Your Doorstep

Presenting 10 Millbrook Apartments — a beautifully maintained 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom duplex apartment with its own private entrance, in the heart of Johnstown, Navan, Co. Meath. This spacious home offers approximately 100m² of well-designed living space, flooded with natural light and finished to a high standard throughout.

The layout is cleverly designed — three good-sized bedrooms and two bathrooms on the ground floor, with the bright open-plan living, kitchen, and dining area on the upper level, taking full advantage of the elevated views. The contemporary kitchen features striking teal cabinetry with integrated appliances, a breakfast bar, and ample counter space.

Recently upgraded with brand new WiFi-enabled smart heaters and a new boiler, the home is energy efficient and comfortable year-round. A south-west facing sun terrace off the living area offers beautiful views of the surrounding green landscape and Johnstown People's Park.

With the NX bus stop just outside the estate, Johnstown Shopping Centre a short walk away, and excellent schools within minutes, this is an ideal home for professionals, commuters, and families alike.

Why Johnstown

The Best of Town & Nature, Combined

Johnstown is a thriving, well-established community on the edge of Navan town. With a population of over 12,000, it has its own schools, shops, park, and sports clubs — everything you need for daily life without ever needing the car. And when you do, Dublin and Blanchardstown are under an hour away.

  • Population of 12,000+ with strong community spirit
  • 8-acre Johnstown People's Park with walking tracks & sports
  • SuperValu, Costa, and local shops within walking distance
  • Primary and secondary schools within minutes
  • NX bus to Dublin city & Blanchardstown at the estate gate
  • M3 Junction 8 just 2.5 km — 25 min to M50
